Chianti Classico Wine Tour from Il Cortile Pratolino

A Wine Adventure from Il Cortile Pratolino

Trip Overview

Enjoy a delightful day trip to the Chianti region, just 45 minutes from Il Cortile Pratolino. Visit Castello di Fonterutoli, a historic winery owned by the Mazzei family since 1435, famous for its Chianti Classico wines. Take a guided vineyard tour, learn about winemaking, and enjoy a tasting of premium wines paired with local cheeses and olive oil. The trip includes a scenic drive through Chianti’s hills and a stop in Radda in Chianti for lunch at a traditional trattoria, featuring dishes like panzanella and bistecca alla fiorentina.

Highlights

  • Guided tour and wine tasting at Castello di Fonterutoli
  • Scenic drive through Chianti’s vineyards and olive groves
  • Lunch at a family-run trattoria in Radda in Chianti
  • Explore the medieval village of Radda

One-Day Chianti Road Trip Itinerary

From Il Cortile Pratolino to Chianti’s Heart

Itinerary Overview

Start: Il Cortile Pratolino, near Montegonzi (9:30 AM)

Destinations: Greve in Chianti, Montefioralle, Lamole, Panzano, Castellina in Chianti, Montegonzi

End: Montegonzi (by 5:00 PM)

Total Driving Distance: ~60 km

Total Driving Time: ~1 hour 45 minutes

Total Time: 7 hours 30 minutes (including activities and lunch)

Detailed Itinerary

9:30 AM – Depart to Greve in Chianti

Drive: 15 km, ~20 min via SR222. From Il Cortile Pratolino, take SP94 to SR222, park near Piazza Matteotti.

Scenic: Vineyards, cypress trees. More info

9:50 AM – 10:50 AM: Greve in Chianti (1 hr)

Stroll Piazza Matteotti, visit Antica Macelleria Falorni for tastings, try Chianti Classico at Enoteca Falorni. Details

10:50 AM – Drive to Montefioralle

Drive: 2 km, ~5 min via Via Roma. Park at Piazza Santo Stefano. Route

11:00 AM – 11:45 AM: Montefioralle (45 min)

Walk medieval streets, see Church of Santo Stefano, taste wine at Montefioralle Winery. Info

11:45 AM – Drive to Lamole

Drive: 8 km, ~15 min via Via Lamole. Park near Ristoro di Lamole. Route

12:15 PM – 1:45 PM: Lunch at Ristoro di Lamole (1 hr 30 min)

Enjoy Tuscan dishes on the terrace. Reserve at +39 055 854 7050. Reviews

1:45 PM – Drive to Panzano

Drive: 6 km, ~10 min via SR222. Park near main square. Route

2:00 PM – 3:00 PM: Panzano (1 hr)

Visit Antica Macelleria Cecchini, explore Panzano Alto, see Pieve di San Leolino. Details

3:00 PM – Drive to Castellina in Chianti

Drive: 15 km, ~20 min via SR222. Park near main square. Route

3:20 PM – 4:20 PM: Castellina in Chianti (1 hr)

Walk Via delle Volte, visit Rocca Fortress, grab gelato at Gelateria Castellina. Info

4:20 PM – Drive to Montegonzi

Drive: 14 km, ~20 min via SR222 and SP99. Route

4:40 PM – Arrive in Montegonzi (by 5:00 PM)

Relax at Il Cortile Pratolino or take a short walk in Montegonzi. Details

Additional Tips

  • Driving: SR222 and side roads are curvy; use a compact car. Tips
  • Parking: Use lots outside ZTL zones; Greve and Castellina have larger spaces. Parking
  • What to Bring: Comfortable shoes, camera, cash for small shops/parking.
  • Reservations: Book Ristoro di Lamole (+39 055 854 7050). Call Montefioralle Winery (+39 055 853 189). Info
  • Seasonal: May offers pleasant weather; check for festivals like I Vini del Castello. Events
  • Fuel: Gas stations at Coop in Greve. Details
